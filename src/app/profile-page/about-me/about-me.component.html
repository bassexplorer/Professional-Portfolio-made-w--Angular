A couple of things about me...

Even though I love electrical engineering (fun fact: In my free time I produce music and I learned a lot about sounds and how we can "synthesize" them) I realized I like programing more. So I wanted to broaden my knowledge and try something new so I did a short course in Web-design. This was the point I realized I want to be work in this field and show to myself I can stand on my feet. I moved to Denmark and started my studies here and my journey to become a developer.

I enjoy everything where I can be creative, it could be music or programming. I always keep in focus the goals and try to achieve the best result I can. I like challenges and be the man who solve them.
